What is SafeRides?

SafeRides is designed exclusively for university and college students, creating a secure and verified ride-sharing community. We believe in peer-to-peer safety, ensuring every ride is with someone you can trust.

  • Student-Only Access: Only verified students can sign up, drive, or ride, fostering a closed and trusted network.
  • Trusted Contact Feature: Share your ride details with a trusted contact who can track your journey in real-time.
  • Female Driver Option: Female riders have the option to request a female driver for added comfort and security.
  • Verified Profiles: All users undergo a thorough verification process to ensure authenticity and safety.

About Us

Our mission at SafeRides is simple yet profound: to provide a secure and reliable transportation solution that empowers students to move freely and safely, especially during late hours.

We understand the concerns associated with late-night travel and the importance of feeling secure. SafeRides was founded on the principle of reducing the risk of riding with strangers by building a community where every member is a verified student. Our goal is to foster an environment of trust and mutual responsibility, ensuring peace of mind for students and their families.

We are committed to continuous improvement, leveraging technology to enhance safety features and user experience, making SafeRides the go-to choice for student transportation.

About Our Founder

Zack Coetzee founded SafeRides after hearing one too many stories of students being put in danger by traditional ride-sharing apps. As a college student himself, Zack saw a gap: students needed a safer, more trusted way to get around—especially at night.

With a background in entrepreneurship and a passion for building real solutions, he created SafeRides: a platform built by students, for students. His mission is simple—make getting home feel safe again.

Insurance to Help Protect You

Whether you're logged on or actively driving, SafeRides ensures the right protection is in place for you and your riders.

Your personal auto insurance covers you when offline. To drive with SafeRides, you must maintain personal insurance at minimum state limits.
When you're online and waiting for a trip, SafeRides provides limited liability:
  • $50,000 per person / $100,000 per accident for injuries
  • $25,000 in property damage coverage

Depending on your state, SafeRides may offer additional protections.

Once you've accepted a ride, you're covered by:
  • $1M in liability coverage
  • Physical damage to your car (with a $2,500 deductible) if you have comprehensive and collision

Additional medical and injury protections may apply based on your location.
